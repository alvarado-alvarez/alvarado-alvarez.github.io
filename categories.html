Undergraduate Seminar

This is the category theory section of the undergraduate seminar MATHUN3952. Please reach out to me with any questions at ca3172 (at) columbia (dot) edu.

We will generally follow Emily Riehl's book "Category Theory in Context" but feel free to let me know if there is a related topic you'd prefer to present on. You can reach me by email to schedule a meeting before your talk if you'd like, and also please let me know if I have miswritten your name or if there is another name you would prefer that I write on the schedule.

Meeting Time and Location: Tuesdays (HAM406)/Thursdays(HAM408) 4:40pm-5:40pm

Course Policy Reminders: Students are expected to attend all meetings and absences must be excused beforehand. Each participant will give 1 or 2 talks over the course of the semester.
Section Policy: Your grade may be impacted after 25% or more unexcused absences. We will only allow chalk talks and you may not use AI to help prepare your talk.
