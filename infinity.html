Infinity Categories
Learning Seminar

This is a learning seminar on the theory of infinity categories. We will cover very quickly some concepts from ordinary category theory to compare them against concepts of higher categories. We will follow Markus Land's book.

    Introduction to Infinity Categories

    Carlos Andrés Alvarado Álvarez

    We will cover the bare minimum of the theory of simplicial sets that is necessary to define an infinity category. We will then compare this to the classical definition of a category and introduce the nerve of a category and the homotopy category.
