Infinity Categories
Learning Seminar

The goal of this seminar is to introduce the theory of Infinity Categories; we will follow Charles Rezk's notes on quasicategories.

There are many constructions and properties that we would like to be have and sometimes this only happens when we use the language of infinity categories. More and more papers are written in the language of infinity categories and it will be good to be familiar with it.

Our main application will be a construction of the stable category and the smash product on spectra; which cannot be properly done with the language of ordinary categories.
