Young R.A.T. Seminar

The main organizer is Azélie Picot, who also came up with the idea for this seminar.
R.A.T. stands for Researchers Are Talking.

The talks should be either about the speakers own research or could also be about a cool result in their field which one might want to present on without organizing a whole reading seminar.

We want this to be a space where students can present their research to others in the department and at the same time practice giving research talks.

We don't necessarily only want algebraic topologists to be the only speakers. However, we would prefer if the speakers came from adjacent fields i.e. algebraic geometry or geometric topology.
